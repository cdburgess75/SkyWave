◆ Antenna calculator

Enter a frequency to compute lengths.
Doublet (ladder-line, multiband): cut as long as the lot allows — 135 ft covers 80–10 m, 88 ft for 40–10 m, 44 ft for 40 m up. Feed with window line into a 4:1 Guanella + tuner.
Coax-fed resonant dipole: 1:1 current balun at the feedpoint. Off-center-fed / folded → 4:1. RG-8X/LMR feedline velocity factor ≈ 0.82–0.86 if you're cutting a phasing/matching section.
